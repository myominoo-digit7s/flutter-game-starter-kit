# Privacy Policy Guide

## Required Placeholders
Your generated Privacy Policy relies on:
- `appName`
- `companyName`
- `contactEmail`
- `effectiveDate`

## How to update policy text
The default text is a basic template in `PrivacyPolicyScreen`. You can replace the Markdown string with your actual finalized policy.

## How to host policy URL
You must host your Privacy Policy on a publicly accessible website (e.g., GitHub Pages, Firebase Hosting, or your company's website) and submit that URL to Google Play and the App Store.

## Reminder
Ensure your final Privacy Policy accurately reflects your SDK usage (e.g., Firebase Crashlytics, AdMob) and Data Safety declarations. If you collect Device Identifiers or Location, your policy must explicitly state this.
