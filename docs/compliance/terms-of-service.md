# Terms of Service Guide

## Required Placeholders
The Terms of Service relies on:
- `appName`
- `companyName`
- `contactEmail`

## Ads / Rewards Section
If `hasAds` is true, ensure your Terms clarify that any in-game rewards received for watching ads have no real-world monetary value.

## Virtual Currency Placeholder
If you add an in-game economy, add a clause stating that virtual coins are licensed, not owned, and cannot be transferred or sold for real money.

## Limitation Disclaimer Placeholder
Ensure the Limitation of Liability section protects your company from damages arising from the use of the app. Consult legal counsel to ensure enforceability in your jurisdiction.
