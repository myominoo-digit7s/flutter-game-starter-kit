# Content Rating Guide

## Content Rating Checklist
Before submitting your app to the store, you must complete the IARC content rating questionnaire.
Review your game for:
- Violence (cartoon, fantasy, realistic)
- Fear/scary content
- Simulated gambling
- Online user interaction
- Location sharing

## Age-Targeting Notes
Be honest. If you mark the game as suitable for all ages but it contains violence or gambling mechanics, your app will be rejected or banned.
